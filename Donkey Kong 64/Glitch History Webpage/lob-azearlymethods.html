Methods of Aztec Lobby Early

INTRO STORY GLITCH TAG BARREL STORAGE MOONKICKS STVW & PHASING LAG ABUSE SEE ALL

MOONTAIL LEDGE CLIP (CHIMPY CHARGE)

The first method of Angry Aztec Lobby Early to be found. This required a moontail and a ledge clip to clip into the archway followed by a precise Chimpy Charge to hit the loading zone.

MOONTAIL LEDGE CLIP (NO CHIMPY CHARGE)

A small improvement on the previous method. At the time, this meant that Chimpy Charge could be delayed until after entering Aztec in Any%.

LEDGE CLIP (SKID JUMP)

This was the first method of Aztec Early that didn't required Diddy. This meant that Jungle Japes could be skipped entirely since you could free Chunky without Diddy. As a result, you could acquire Key 5 and obtain Diddy & Tiny via Kut Out.

LEDGE CLIP (CEILING KICK)

An improvement for RTA over the skid jump method. This replaced the skid jump with a ceiling kick to reach the loading zone.

LAG CLIP (AS DK)

With the recent discovery of orange clips to bypass collision, this paved the way to an easier method of Angry Aztec Lobby Early. This method is still used to this day for N64 to clip past the door.

LAG CLIP (AS DIDDY)

Whilst similar in principle to the DK method, this uses Chimpy Charge to get a good speed to lag clip through the door as Diddy.

DAMAGE BOOST

This uses both the lag from orange explosions and the boost from the damage to clip through the door.

SKEW

This method uses damage skew with a beaver to get skew in DK Isles. Upon doing this, you can take either Warp 2 or climb up to Aztec and clip through the wall.

ISG (WRONG CUTSCENE)

Intro Story Glitch (ISG) is a very powerful glitch which can recall cutscenes 0,1,7 and 8 in a map with some precise timing. Using this, you can recall cutscene 8 in the DK Isles map to Open Aztec without Key 1.

GLORIOUS BOOSTING

After the advent of Tag Barrel Storage and warping to stored positions, it was discovered that you could gain height by repeatedly warping to a stored position and then jump-aerialing and pausing to set the stored position slightly higher.
With this, you can slowly gain height to enter Angry Aztec Lobby from below.

BOULDER PUSH

By picking up and placing down a boulder repeatedly with Chunky's back against a wall, you can push yourself through the wall. With this, you can push yourself through the Aztec Lobby structure and into the loading zone.

PHASE WALKING

Phase Walking introduced a way to clip Out of Bounds in a similar way to 'Swim through Vertical Walls'. Using this, you can clip into the Aztec Lobby structure, and enter the loading zone.
