WRONG CUTSCENE

By causing a fadeout into the Factory K Rool Intro, and then entering the loading zone into DK Isles, the 'Open Castle Lobby' Cutscene plays without needing Key 5.

Getting this to occur is a lot harder and requires Tag Barrel Storage, Portal Storage, and very specific positioning.
