Methods of Factory Lobby Early

INTRO STORY GLITCH TAG BARREL STORAGE MOONKICKS STVW & PHASING LAG ABUSE SEE ALL

ROCKETBARREL

By performing Fungi Early, then entering the rocketbarrel, you can reach near the Loading zone to Frantic Factory Lobby. In conjunction with Castle Kongs to free Chunky, you can enter Factory Lobby without Key 2

MOONKICK (SLOPE KICK)

By performing a moonkick near the entrance to Gloomy Galleon Lobby, you can get to DK Caged GB Platform. With this, you can kick onto the ramp to Frantic Factory with only DK

MOONTAIL

Using a moontail, you can reach the ledge to the ramp to Frantic Factory. As a result, you can reach Frantic Factory Lobby as Diddy Only

MOONKICK (CEILING KICK)

By clipping into either box that connects K-Lumsy's Isle and Kroc Isle, you can perform a ceiling kick, which can allow you to get onto the ramp.

ULTRA-LAG

By building up a crazy amount of lag, you can travel long distances without losing too much height. As a result, you can go to a high point on the other side of Kroc Isle, and ultra lag kick to the ramp.

MONKEYPORT

Possibly the simplest method of them all. If you acquire monkeyport early (With MMM or by entering Caves early), you can use Monkeyportto reach the top of K-Rool Island, then head down to Factory.

You can also lag through the ship to enter the loading zone quicker (as shown in the video), but it's not mandatory.
