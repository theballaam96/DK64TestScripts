ROCK STACKING

The original method of Caves Lobby Early found in mid 2009. This involves stacking 2 rocks on the slope below the Caves Lobby entrance, then backflipping as Tiny to grab the ledge to enter Caves Early.

PONY TAIL TWIRL

Found shortly after the rock stacking method, this only uses a Pony Tail Twirl to grab the ledge to clip through the rock into Caves Lobby.

MOONKICK

One of the many applications of the recently discovered (at the time) 'moonkick'. This method is the one still used to this day.

CEILING KICK

By ledge clipping into the stairs near Angry Aztec, you can perform a ceiling kick to get directly to the Caves Lobby loading zone without having to consider the rock.
